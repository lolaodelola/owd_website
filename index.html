Web platform documentation is critical digital infrastructure.

We are a community of web developers, standards makers, and technology companies that considers web platform documentation to be critical digital infrastructure, and we work cooperatively to ensure its long-term health.

We are funded by corporate and individual donations. To see who has contributed, or to contribute yourself, see our Open Collective page.

Currently our work is focussed on creating and maintaining content for MDN Web Docs.
